French Fries

Description

French fries are THE staple side for a burger. These homemade french fries are sure to make your mouth water!

Ingredients

  • 800 grams potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes then cut them into long, 8mm thick french fries.
  2. Soak the fries in water for at least 30 minutes. Drain them throughly, then pat fries dry with kitchen paper.
  3. Heat airfryer to 320 degrees F (160 degrees C).
  4. Put the fries in a large bowl, drizzle with the oil and toss to coat. Transfer them to airfryer basket. Slide basket into airfryer and set timer for 18 minutes.
  5. When timer rings, slide basket out and shake fries. Adjust temperature to 356 degrees F (180 degrees C) and set timer for another 12 minutes.
  6. After 6 minutes, slide out basket and shake fries again.
  7. Fry until the timer rings and fries are golden brown. Sprinkle with salt and serve.
